Beneficial for Foodies

Helpful for all people interested in making new food items

Veg/Non-Veg Cuisines

Recommends food according to your choice!

Usage

In this website depending on the vegetables, species and other eating materials in user’s fridge, the food items and their recipes are suggested. The user will enter ingredients which he has available with him at the moment and the app will recommend some recipes that the user can cook from the available ingredients.

Objective

Objective of this website is to help outstation students and office workers to cook food with the materials that they have available thus saving them the worry of deciding what to cook.
We are using a dataset which contains recipes of many dishes.As the dataset also contains the approximate time required to cook the dish, it becomes more user-friendly.

Scope

Students and office workers who live away from their homes due to studies and work respectively face the problem of knowing what and how to cook.
So using this website, they will get every help they’ll need regarding the matter.
It is also helpful to all the housewives who want instant recipe recommendations from the ingredients that they currently have.
The scope of this website is directed towards students and office workers living away from their homes.
The scope can also be extended to normal people who want to try cooking some other cuisine with the ingredients that they have available.
