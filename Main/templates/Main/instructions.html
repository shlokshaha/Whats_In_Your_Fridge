How to Use this Website?

    Below are the steps to be followed to use this website...
  • If you don't have an account, Register your account.
  • If you already have an account, Login using your username and password.
  • After logging in into the site, you will be directed to the Input Page. Here you have to select your meal preferences and submit the form.
  • According to your meal preferences (Veg/Non-Veg & Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner) you will be directed to the ingredients page.
  • On the ingredients page (Veg/Non-Veg), you will be provided with the list of various ingredients/vegetables.
  • Select all the ingredients/vegetables you currently have for better recipe recommendations.
  • After selecting the ingredients submit the form. You will be redirected to the results(final) page.
  • The Result page will contain the list of different recipes you can prepare based on the ingredients you filled up in the previous pages.
  • Also, don't forget to give your valuable feedback to us. The link for which will be provided on the respective pages.
